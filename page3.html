A concept UX case study focused on enhancing restaurant efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Overview

Rencia Foods is a restaurant that experiences crowding and delays during peak hours. This concept app aims to help the business manage seating capacity efficiently while ensuring customers are served promptly with their preferred dishes available.

Problem

Customers often arrive to find no free tables or face long waiting times before being served. The restaurant struggles to optimize its seating capacity and kitchen readiness simultaneously, leading to frustration and missed opportunities for both staff and customers.

Goals

  • Business goal: Manage space efficiently and reduce customer disappointment.
  • Customer goals: Get served quickly on arrival and always find preferred dishes available.

Research

Initial user insights were drawn from casual conversations with diners and restaurant staff. Common issues included long wait times, unavailable dishes, and uncertainty about seating. These insights guided the app‚Äôs design priorities.

Key Insights

  • Users want real-time table availability before arrival.
  • Updated menu availability builds customer trust.
  • Simple reservation flows reduce frustration.

Proposed Solution

A mobile application that provides real-time table tracking, allows quick reservations, and displays live menu availability. The solution ensures that both staff and customers experience smoother, faster interactions within the restaurant environment.

Expected Outcome

  • Reduced waiting time upon arrival.
  • Improved customer satisfaction through reliability.
  • Optimized table turnover and kitchen efficiency.
