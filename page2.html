A concept UX case study focused on enhancing restaurant efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Overview

Rencia Foods app is a restaurant concept designed for my family newly opened restaurant to manage space efficiently and minimize customer waiting times. This app ensures customers are served quickly and that their preferred dishes are always available.

Problem Definition

Customers often find no free tables or wait long before being served. Operational inefficiencies in seating and kitchen timing lead to customer dissatisfaction and revenue loss.

Goals

Business Goals

  • Optimize seating and reduce disappointment.
  • Boost customer retention with faster service.

Customer Goals

  • Get served quickly after arrival.
  • Always find favorite meals available.

Research Insights

Feedback and Observation from diners and staff revealed pain points like unpredictable wait times and unavailable meals. This guided the design toward transparency and real-time communication.

Proposed Solution

A mobile application that enables customers to check table availability, reserve seats, and see live menu updates. Designed to streamline staff coordination and customer flow.

Expected Outcome

  • Reduced waiting time upon arrival.
  • Improved customer satisfaction.
  • Enhanced operational efficiency.
